About Us

Titan Aluminium Fabricators Ltd is a leading provider of premium aluminium solutions in Kenya. With years of experience, we specialize in the design, supply, and installation of modern, durable, and sleek aluminium windows, doors, curtain walls, partitioning, shop fronts, staircases, balconies etc.

  • Skilled and passionate team
  • Over 10 years of industry experience
  • Trusted by hundreds of clients

To design, supply, and install premium aluminium solutions that combine quality, innovation, and durability—enhancing the beauty and value of projects, homes, and businesses across East Africa.
